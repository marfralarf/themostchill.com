Willful Whims



In Willful Whims, you play as a Dreamer, a child with psionic powers which are most strongly apparent while dreaming. Dreamers retain full lucidity in their dreams and will engage in playful combat with other Dreamers by commanding everything from people to animals to common (and extraordinary) objects to battle using their powerful Whims.

You will have 3 types of cards: a Dreamer card which represents the child who commands their own dream domain
10 Figment cards which represent the many bizarre actors which populate the Dreamer's Dreamscape
and 40 Whim cards which represent the Dreamer's ability to influence the events of the Dreamscape.
